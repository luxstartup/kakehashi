"Phantom" Brulee Baumkuchen Returns:
Crispy! Melty! One Day Only.

【1/24限定】パリッ！トロッ！年に一度の幻のスイーツ

Taiyo-sha Co., Ltd. (Chiba, Japan) announces the release of its legendary "Brulee Baumkuchen" for one day only on January 24, 2026. Known for selling out instantly every year, this year's edition features a "Strawberry Custard" filling for the first time in the series history.

"Paris! Mochi! Toro! Shari!"
(Crispy! Chewy! Melty! Crunchy!)

Experience a symphony of textures that change with every bite. The savory caramelized top meets the sweet and sour strawberry filling in a limited flavor available only for 2026.

Why is it so special? (3 Secrets)
POINT 01

The "Double Brulee" Method

Most shops torch the sugar once. Taiyo-sha does it twice.

By baking the caramel layer in two stages, we create a distinct thickness and depth of bitterness. This results in a clear "Paris!" (Crispy) sound when you insert your knife—a texture impossible to achieve with a single firing.

POINT 02

Overflowing Strawberry Custard

Hidden beneath the cracked brulee is a milky, rich custard mixed with Strawberry Puree. Unlike raw strawberries, cooking them eliminates any grassy odor, leaving only a pure, sweet-sour aroma. The cream spills out ("Toro!") the moment it is cut.

POINT 03

Limited Red & Gold Package

To celebrate this one-day event, the product comes in a special luxurious box featuring a red and gold motif. It is designed to be the perfect winter gift or a treat for yourself.
