To Our Luxembourg Readers

Why travel 9,000km for 'Nothing'?

In Luxembourg, we live fast. We are the heart of Europe, busy, efficient, and always connected. But Japan offers something we have lost: 'Ma' (間) — the beauty of empty space and silence. This Kominka isn't just a hotel; it's a time machine that takes you back to a simpler, quieter earth. It is the ultimate detox for the busy European soul.

Japan is not just Tokyo and Kyoto. True luxury lies in the deep silence of the mountains. 'SATOHYAMA' is a private villa limited to one group per day, renovated from a feudal-era folk house. Here, there are no TVs, no clocks. Only the sound of falling snow and the warmth of the irori hearth.

Unfiltered History

The pillars are black with soot from 150 years of fires. The designers dared not to paint over them. Modern comforts (floor heating, Simmons beds) are invisibly integrated, preserving the rugged beauty of the past while ensuring absolute comfort.

“We sell time, not rooms.”

The Snow Onsen

Open the sliding glass doors, and the freezing mountain air hits your face, but your body remains wrapped in 41°C natural spring water. The 'Yukimi-buro' (snow-viewing bath) is a quintessential Japanese winter experience that cleanses the soul.
